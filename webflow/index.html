FAQs

Why wouldn 't I just hire a full-time designer?

Good question! Initially, it 's worth noting that the yearly expense for a senior designer is now over £75k in the UK and above $100k in the US, inclusive of perks. Moreover, there might not always be a consistent flow of tasks for them, leading to underutilisation of their time, which you still have to pay for. The monthly subscription model offers flexibility, allowing you to halt and restart as required, ensuring you 're only incurring costs when there 's actual work for the designer.

Is there a limit to how many requests I can have?

After subscribing, you can populate your queue with numerous design tasks, which will be addressed sequentially. However, if you opt for the pro plan, you 'll benefit from having two tasks processed simultaneously.

How fast will I receive my designs?

Typically, the majority of requests are fulfilled within a two-day timeframe or even sooner. That said, intricate tasks like branding, website, and app designs might require a bit more time.

How will we communicate?

We 'll collaborate through a unified project board. Here, you 'll see all our deliverables and what we expect from you in return. Direct messages and feedback can be exchanged right within the project. And if you wish, you can also bring in other team members.

Who are the designers?

It might catch you off guard, but DesignShip is a one-person show. This means you 'll be directly liaising with me CJ, the brain behind DesignShip. For enhanced services like animations or unique illustrations, we team up with our affiliated designers.

How do I request designs?

DesignShip offers a ton of flexibility in how you request designs using Trello. Some common ways clients request designs is directly via Trello, sharing Google docs or wireframes, or even recording a brief Loom video (for those who prefer not to write their briefs out). Basically, if it can be linked to or shared in Trello, it 's fair game.

What if I don 't like the designs?

No drama! We 'll continue to revise the design until you 're 100% satisfied.

How does the pause feature work?

We recognize that you might not always have a month 's worth of design tasks. Maybe you just have a couple of design needs right now. This is where the option to pause your subscription becomes beneficial. Our billing cycles run on a 31-day basis. For instance, if you register and utilize the service for 21 days and then opt to pause, the billing will also halt. Consequently, you 'll retain 10 days of unused service, which can be accessed whenever you wish in the future.

What if I only have one request?

Absolutely. Once you 're done, feel free to pause your subscription and come back when you have more design tasks. Rest assured, the remaining days of your subscription won 't be squandered.

Are there any refunds if I don 't like the service?

Due to the high quality nature of the work, there will be no refunds issued.
